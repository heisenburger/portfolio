Based in the coolest little capital in the world: Wellington, New Zealand.

Hello fellow human! I live for interesting and engaging conversations, so don't be shy. If you're in Wellington, or not, hit me up over twitter or email and we can grab a coffee, tea, or beverage of your choice.

This was a passion project that stemmed from a frustration with the lack of high-quality media for teens (especially teen girls), as well as the drive to change the negative media landscape, by focusing on things such as body positivity, intersectionality and openness.
