Fast, lightweight, functional.

Prioritising the last-minute lookups, the on-the-go drop bys, the lunchtime breaks. Our locator focused on minimising cruft and maximising speed.

When you focus on performance, some assumed classics just don't make the bar. Embedded, fully interactive maps, for example, were simply too large, slow, and cumbersome on mobile devices. Instead, we opted for a static map from Google Maps, with the option of opening your native mapping application a primary action.

Starting with features from the old locator site, we began cleaning house.
